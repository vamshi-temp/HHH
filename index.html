What is Hemochromatosis?

Iron Overload 101

HH causes excessive iron absorption.

Who's at Risk?

Common in Irish/North-European ancestry but can affect anyone.

Symptoms

Fatigue, joint pain, liver issues, diabetes.

Treatment

Simple blood tests + regular donation keep iron balanced.
