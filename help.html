How You Can Help

Help us turn hemochromatosis from a health problem into something that can save lives.

Ways to Make a Difference

Donate Blood

If you have HH, your blood draws can save lives. Schedule regular donations at Red Cross centers instead of discarding your blood.

Find Centers

Spread Awareness

Share information about HH symptoms and the importance of early detection. Every person you inform could prevent serious complications.

Get Tested

If you have Northern European ancestry or HH symptoms, get a simple ferritin test. Early detection is key to preventing organ damage.

Help Others Get Tested

One of the best ways to help is encouraging others to get tested for hemochromatosis. There are multiple testing options available including Amazon home test kits and professional lab services like HealthyIron.
