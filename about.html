About The Iron Foundation

Transforming hereditary hemochromatosis from a curse into a cure, one donation at a time.

Our Mission

Awareness

Teaching people about hemochromatosis symptoms and getting tested early with simple blood work.

Connection

Connecting HH patients with Red Cross donation centers where their blood draws can save lives instead of being discarded.

Empowerment

Helping people with iron overload see how their condition can actually help others who need blood.

Research

Supporting research and advocacy to help doctors diagnose HH better and improve care for patients.

Our Origin Story

The Iron Foundation was built on a simple, but urgent mission: to raise awareness about Hemochromatosis and bring to attention the ease of donation through the Red Cross.

Inspired by the potential to save lives, we urge you to donate your therapeutic blood which is gold to others. Through early detection and treatment, our initiative combines storytelling, education, and action through one powerful movement.

What started as a personal journey with hereditary hemochromatosis has grown into a movement that transforms individual health challenges into collective healing power.
